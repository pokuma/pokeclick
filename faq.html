It depends on the pokemon. Each pokemon gives different xp amount based on Oficial Pokemon stats.

Game doesn't have a limit. Your goal is to catch all the 151 pokemon but you can still play to get more levels, upgrades or catch shiny pokemons!

Each pokemon has a different catch rate based on official Pokemon stats, so a legendary pokemon like Mew is harder to catch than a Caterpie.

The damage over time is exactly the same than click damage per second.

It appears on the pokedex panel and also on the top of the screen at the Pokedex counter. Whenever you encounter a pokemon that you have already catched, the pokeball icon on top of the pokemon name will be filled.

You get more click damage (so damage over time) each time you level up.
