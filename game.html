pokedex


this is a game where your goal is to catch all the 151 pokemon.

everytime a pokemon is defeated, a pokeball is automatically thrown, and the pokemon will have a chance to be caught.

each pokemon has a different catch rate, so you will probably have to defeat some pokemon multiple times to catch it.


press any key to or the "next" button to continue.
